Website Evaluation - The Local

1. Website URL

https://www.thelocalharco.com

2. Website Name

The Local

3. Target Audience

The Local aims to attract diners in Harford County and surrounding areas who are seeking a farm-to-table dining experience that emphasizes locally sourced ingredients.

4. Site Organization

The website features a clear and intuitive navigation menu with sections including:

  • Menu (Lunch, Dinner, Brunch, Happy Hour & Daily Specials, Cocktails, Mocktails & More, Wine, Dessert, Catering)
  • Reservations
  • Gift Cards
  • Our Team
  • Our Local Community
  • Accolades and Press
  • Home

5. CRAP Design Principles

The website effectively employs the CRAP design principles including Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity.

6. Accessibility Audit Score

The site's audit score is 85%.

7. Site Effectiveness

The site effectively supports users in completing actions accurately, providing detailed information.

8. Site Efficiency

Users can perform tasks quickly due to the responsive design and intuitive navigation.

9. Engagement

The website provides a pleasant user experience with a clean design and relevant content.

10. Recommendation for Improvement

To enhance accessibility, consider increasing color contrast and ensuring all forms are accessible.
